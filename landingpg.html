About US

The creation of online art gallary was the brain child of it's founder and together with
the core team, their prime focus is to increase awareness of spirituality in a way that
would engage both children and adults.There is a famous English saying: “a picture
is worth a thousand words”. Paintings are more than just pictures in a frame,
they are a kaleidoscope of stories generating numerous discussions and perspectives.
Our artwork aspires to spark such discussions through mindfulness, thoughtfulness,
and spiritual awareness.We pride ourselves on having carefully selected a
faction of talented spiritually-minded artists from different corners of the world,
producing art exclusively for art-ma. Our focal point is to promote both emerging
and established artists, who have a strong focus on spirituality; stand for a
particular cause; or have a defined goal in life.At art-ma, we make art accessible
to all. Please explore the different price points available at our online store.
